The Future of Public Transportation.

Our innovative route planning app, commuter, aims to increase the efficacy of the public transit system by facilitating effective planning via sustainable transportation. This app will also incorporate educational components specific to respective regional transit systems to help overcome the hurdles often associated with figuring out how to use these systems. By increasing consumer interactions with public transit system, the goal is to generate a net positive environmental impact by diverting people away from unsustainable single-occupancy vehicles.

Features on the app:
◆ contact button to receive more information
◆ “Your city” page tab (this would have educational info for particular region
◆ “your route” page tab (this would have fields where they can enter where they’re going)
◆ on the home page: logo, map image, idk what else
